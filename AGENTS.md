# Agent Guidelines

## Design Principles

- **Blockquotes:** Blockquotes in all articles should have an orange background (`#e67e22`) with white text to stand out, and a large white quote icon.
- **Image Captions:** In all layouts, image captions (legends) must be positioned closer/flush to the photo (`margin-top` around `0.25rem` and `margin-bottom` around `0` on the image), smaller in size (`font-size` around `13px` or `0.875rem`), and displayed in *italics*.
- **Post Footer:** The post footer (tags, edit button, Google News link, share buttons, author box) must follow a consistent layout structure as defined in `PostFooter.tsx`.
- **Post Hero Image Position:** The main hero image (capa) in all posts MUST be positioned at the top of the content container (under breadcrumbs) and BEFORE the `<header>` block that contains the tags, title, subtitle, author, and date. The image should be full-width with `rounded-xl`, `border border-[#311B92]/50`, and `shadow-[0_10px_25px_rgba(0,0,0,0.5)]`.
- **Readability:** Break large chunks of text into smaller, skimmable paragraphs. Avoid grouping many sentences together if they can be separated, but don't isolate very short lines unnecessarily.
- **Article Ending:** After the main article text and responsible gaming notices, always include "Ler o Próximo" (Read Next) and "Artigos Relacionados" (Related Articles) sections.
- **Guides & Analyses Layout:** The layout for guides and analyses sections must follow this exact sequence: 
  1 rectangular featured post -> 4 or 5 smaller boxes -> 2 larger square boxes side-by-side -> 4 or 5 smaller boxes.
- **Hero/Slider Updates:** Any newly published article (whether from Sanity.io or directly in the AI Studio) must be featured in the Hero/Slider section, keeping the 5 most recent publications in rotation. In addition, update the "Guias, Análises & Dicas Master" section, the "ARTIGOS POPULARES" block in post pages, and the "Guias, Análises e Dicas" block in Sidebar 2.
- **Feed Layout (Locked CSS/Structure):** The DfolgaBet feed layout (`DfolgaBetLiveMatches.tsx`) MUST preserve a 3-column grid structure on desktop (`grid-template-columns: 280px minmax(0, 1fr) 320px`) with `.dfolgabet-left-sidebar` and `.dfolgabet-right-sidebar` tracking alongside the main feed. The inner wrappers (`.dfolgabet-left-sidebar-inner`, `.dfolgabet-right-sidebar-inner`) MUST have `position: sticky; top: 96px; height: fit-content; max-height: none; overflow: visible; align-self: start;` and this CSS cannot be altered or overwritten.
- **FAQ Layout:** To display FAQs, use a list of cards styling each with `bg-[#120826] border border-[#311B92] rounded-xl p-4 md:p-6 mb-4`. The question is bold white (`text-white font-bold mb-2`), the answer is `text-[#b0b0b0] text-[15px] leading-relaxed`. Do NOT use "P:" or "R:" prefixes for questions and answers. The heading "Perguntas Frequentes" should use `<Info className="text-[#e67e22]" />` instead of `<Zap>`.
- **Markets/Odds Layout:** For odds/markets tips (Palpites e Dicas de Apostas), use cards styled with `bg-[#120826] border border-[#311B92] rounded-xl p-4 md:p-6 mb-4`. The title inside should be `text-white font-bold text-lg mb-3`. The text descriptions should be wrapped in `text-[#b0b0b0] text-[15px] leading-relaxed space-y-4`. Critically, the "Palpite Principal" card MUST have an orange left border and a slightly darker background: `border-l-4 border-l-[#e67e22] bg-[#0A051A]/80`. Other tip cards (like "Dica 1", "Dica 2") use the standard card styling. Ensure the section title uses `<ShieldCheck className="text-[#e67e22]" />`.
- **Aviso Legal:** At the end of every post's content, add the Disclaimer (Aviso Legal) box with a left orange border: `mt-8 border-l-4 border-l-[#e67e22] bg-[#0A051A]/50 p-6 rounded-r-xl`.
- **Jogo Responsável:** Underneath the Aviso Legal, render the `<ResponsibleGamingNotice />` component, which has a cyan left border (`border-l-4 border-l-[#50C0CC]`).
- **Lista / Bullets:** All list items (`<ul className="list-disc ...">`) MUST use the `marker:text-[#50C0CC]` class to make bullets cyan (#50C0CC).
- **YouTube Links:** Any YouTube URL pasted or linked within article content MUST automatically render as an embedded video player or thumbnail (iframe) instead of a plain link. The embed should be visually consistent with the rest of the site (e.g. `aspect-video rounded-xl border border-[#311B92] shadow-lg`).
- **H2 / Titles Decorator:** Every `<h2>` section title should have a horizontal purple divider below it to provide visual hierarchy. Specifically, use empty div like: `<div className="h-[1px] w-full bg-[#311B92] mb-6"></div>` directly underneath the `<h2>`.
